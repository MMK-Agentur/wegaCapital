    About

    The Entrepreneurial Investor for Growth

    WENVEST Capital is a private investment firm, founded by Bernd Wendeln. Having started with traditional Angel investments, WENVEST Capital emerged to become an entrepreneurial Venture Capital investor for Growth. The team of WENVEST Capital is dedicated to support the digital leaders of tomorrow with capital, know-how and network. 

    We are based in Munich, Germany and are looking for investment opportunities primarily in Germany but also across other European countries. 
